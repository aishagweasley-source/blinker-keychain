## The components i use: 
- 16 led (as the leds)
- 330 ohm resistors (for the leds)
- 1000 ohm resistor (for the 555 timer)
- polarized capacitor (charging and discharging cycle)
- potentiometer ( to adjust how fast the leds blink)
- 555 timer (make repeating signals)
- battery (power supply)
- switch (to turn on off)

## The wiring design logic:
- using the 555 timer, it will generate repeating signals connected to the leds
- Then I use the resistors and capacitor to adjust the voltagage 
- I also used the switch push button to turn on and off the circuit
